Quick Overview

MoveDrome is Movement's native ve(3,3) DEX, combining Curve's vote-escrowed tokenomics with Solidly's innovations to create sustainable liquidity and fair token distribution.

Built from the ground up for Movement, MoveDrome aligns incentives between traders, liquidity providers, and token holders through its innovative gauge voting and bribe marketplace system.

Key Features

  • ve(3,3) tokenomics model
  • Gauge voting for emissions
  • Bribe marketplace
  • Stable and volatile pools
  • Concentrated liquidity options
  • Protocol owned liquidity

How to Use MoveDrome

Prerequisites

  • Movement wallet
  • Understanding of ve(3,3) mechanics
  • Tokens for liquidity or DROME for voting
  • Long-term perspective for ve positions

Getting Started

  1. 1

    Provide Liquidity

    Add liquidity to stable or volatile pools to earn trading fees and DROME emissions.

  2. 2

    Lock DROME

    Lock DROME tokens for veDROME NFTs, with lock periods from 1 week to 4 years.

  3. 3

    Vote on Gauges

    Use veDROME voting power to direct emissions to your preferred liquidity pools.

  4. 4

    Earn Bribes

    Collect bribes from protocols incentivizing votes for their pools.

  5. 5

    Compound Rewards

    Claim fees, bribes, and rebases weekly. Relock for continued benefits.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Longer locks receive more voting power
  • Vote for pools you provide liquidity to
  • Monitor bribe APRs for best returns
  • Consider protocol partnerships for vetokens
