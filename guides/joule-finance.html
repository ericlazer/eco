Quick Overview

Joule Finance is a comprehensive DeFi platform offering cross-chain bridging, lending markets, and leveraged yield farming. As the liquidity hub for Aptos and Movement, it enables advanced DeFi strategies.

Through LayerZero integration, Joule connects multiple ecosystems, allowing seamless asset transfers and participation in Move-based DeFi with up to 5x leverage on yield farming positions.

Key Features

  • Cross-chain bridge via LayerZero
  • Money markets for lending/borrowing
  • Leveraged yield farming up to 5x
  • Liquidity anchors for efficiency
  • Efficiency mode for stables
  • Isolated lending positions

How to Use Joule Finance

Prerequisites

  • Aptos or Movement wallet
  • APT or MOVE for gas
  • Assets to lend or farm
  • Understanding of leverage risks

Getting Started

  1. 1

    Connect Wallet

    Connect Petra, Martian, or any Aptos/Movement compatible wallet.

  2. 2

    Bridge Assets

    Use LayerZero bridge to transfer assets from EVM chains if needed.

  3. 3

    Supply Collateral

    Deposit assets to earn interest and enable borrowing power.

  4. 4

    Borrow Assets

    Borrow against collateral maintaining safe health factor.

  5. 5

    Leverage Farm

    Use borrowed assets to create leveraged yield farming positions.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Start with 2x leverage maximum
  • Monitor health factor closely
  • Use efficiency mode for stable pairs
  • Set up position alerts

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the liquidation penalty?

5% liquidation penalty plus a 2.5% fee to liquidators. Always maintain safe health factor above 1.2.

How does efficiency mode work?

When borrowing similar assets (like stables), you get higher LTV ratios due to lower volatility risk.
