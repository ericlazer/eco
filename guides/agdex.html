Frequently Asked Questions

What happens at liquidation?

Positions are liquidated when margin falls below maintenance level (0.5%). A 0.6% liquidation fee applies.

Are there trading fees?

Maker: 0.02%, Taker: 0.06%. Volume discounts available for high-volume traders.
