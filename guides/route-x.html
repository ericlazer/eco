Quick Overview

Route-X is a next-generation cross-chain bridge that enables seamless asset transfers between Movement and other major blockchains. With sub-minute transfers and military-grade security, it's the fastest bridge in the ecosystem.

Using innovative liquidity pools and optimistic validation, Route-X provides near-instant bridging while maintaining the highest security standards. It's the primary gateway for assets entering and leaving the Movement ecosystem.

Key Features

  • Sub-minute cross-chain transfers
  • Support for 15+ major blockchains
  • Optimistic validation for speed
  • Built-in liquidity pools
  • MEV-resistant design
  • Emergency pause mechanism

How to Use Route-X

Prerequisites

  • Wallets on source and destination chains
  • Tokens to bridge
  • Gas tokens on both chains
  • Understanding of bridge risks

Getting Started

  1. 1

    Connect Wallets

    Connect wallets for both source and destination chains. Route-X supports MetaMask, WalletConnect, and more.

  2. 2

    Select Route

    Choose source chain, destination chain, and token amount. Route-X shows fees and estimated time.

  3. 3

    Initiate Transfer

    Approve token spend and initiate the bridge transaction. You'll receive a tracking ID.

  4. 4

    Wait for Confirmation

    Most transfers complete in 30-60 seconds. Track progress in real-time on the dashboard.

  5. 5

    Claim Tokens

    Tokens arrive automatically on Movement. For other chains, you may need to claim manually.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Bridge during off-peak hours for lower fees
  • Keep some native tokens on destination chain
  • Use the fee estimator before large transfers
  • Enable notifications for transfer updates

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if a transfer fails?

Failed transfers are automatically refunded to the source address within 24 hours, minus gas fees.

Are there transfer limits?

Yes, limits vary by token and chain, typically $10k for first-time users, increasing with usage history.
