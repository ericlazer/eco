Quick Overview

Mosaic is a cutting-edge DEX aggregator that finds the best trading routes across all decentralized exchanges on Movement. It optimizes trades for the best prices, lowest slippage, and minimal gas costs.

Built specifically for the Movement ecosystem, Mosaic aggregates liquidity from multiple DEXs to ensure traders always get the best execution. Its smart routing algorithm considers gas costs, slippage, and price impact across all available liquidity sources.

Key Features

  • Multi-DEX aggregation on Movement
  • Smart routing for optimal prices
  • Gas cost optimization
  • MEV protection built-in
  • One-click cross-DEX arbitrage
  • Real-time liquidity monitoring

How to Use Mosaic

Prerequisites

  • Movement-compatible wallet
  • MOVE tokens for gas fees
  • Tokens to trade
  • Basic understanding of DEX trading

Getting Started

  1. 1

    Connect Wallet

    Visit mosaic.fi and connect your Movement-compatible wallet (MetaMask, Petra, etc.)

  2. 2

    Select Tokens

    Choose the tokens you want to swap from and to using the intuitive interface.

  3. 3

    Review Routes

    Mosaic will show you the best route across multiple DEXs with estimated output and gas costs.

  4. 4

    Approve & Swap

    Approve the token spend (first time only) and execute the swap with one click.

  5. 5

    Track Transaction

    Monitor your transaction in real-time and receive tokens in your wallet.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Use limit orders for better prices on large trades
  • Enable MEV protection for sensitive transactions
  • Check multiple routes for complex token pairs
  • Set slippage tolerance based on market volatility

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Mosaic find the best price?

Mosaic queries all major DEXs on Movement in real-time and calculates the optimal path considering price, gas, and slippage.

Are there any fees?

Mosaic charges a small 0.1% fee on trades, which is often offset by the better prices found through aggregation.
