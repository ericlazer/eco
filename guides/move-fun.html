Quick Overview

Move Fun is Movement's dedicated meme token launchpad where anyone can create, launch, and trade meme coins with built-in liquidity, anti-rug mechanisms, and community features.

Inspired by pump.fun but built for Movement's high-speed infrastructure, Move Fun makes meme coin creation accessible while protecting traders with forced liquidity locks and fair launch mechanics.

Key Features

  • One-click meme coin creation
  • Automatic liquidity provision
  • Built-in rug protection
  • Fair launch mechanics
  • Community voting system
  • Integrated social features
