Quick Overview

Helix is a revolutionary restaking protocol that allows users to secure multiple blockchain networks simultaneously while earning compounded rewards from all chains.

By leveraging cross-chain technology and liquid staking derivatives, Helix enables users to maximize their staking yields across ecosystems without sacrificing liquidity or security.

Key Features

  • Multi-chain simultaneous staking
  • Liquid staking derivatives (hTokens)
  • Auto-compounding rewards
  • Cross-chain yield optimization
  • Slash insurance options
  • Governance participation across chains

How to Use Helix

Prerequisites

  • Tokens to stake (ETH, MOVE, SOL, etc.)
  • Understanding of staking risks
  • Multi-chain wallet setup
  • Minimum stake amounts vary by chain

Getting Started

  1. 1

    Connect Multi-Chain Wallet

    Connect wallets for each chain you want to stake on. Helix supports all major wallets.

  2. 2

    Select Staking Strategy

    Choose from conservative, balanced, or aggressive multi-chain staking strategies.

  3. 3

    Deposit Assets

    Deposit your tokens and receive liquid hTokens representing your staked position.

  4. 4

    Earn & Compound

    Watch your rewards accumulate from multiple chains. Enable auto-compounding for maximum yields.

  5. 5

    Use hTokens in DeFi

    Use your liquid hTokens in other DeFi protocols while continuing to earn staking rewards.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Diversify across chains to minimize risk
  • Use hTokens as collateral for additional yield
  • Monitor validator performance regularly
  • Consider slash insurance for large positions

Frequently Asked Questions

What are hTokens?

hTokens are liquid representations of your staked assets that can be used in DeFi while earning staking rewards.

Can I unstake immediately?

Yes, by swapping hTokens on DEXs. Direct unstaking follows each chain's unbonding period.
