Quick Overview

Laniakea is a massive multiplayer online RPG that combines AAA gaming quality with blockchain technology, featuring a player-driven economy where every item is a tradeable NFT.

Built on high-performance blockchain infrastructure, Laniakea offers stunning graphics, immersive gameplay, and true ownership of in-game assets, allowing players to earn while enjoying a premium gaming experience.

Key Features

  • AAA graphics quality
  • Player-owned economy
  • Cross-platform play
  • NFT items and land
  • Guild warfare system
  • Play-to-earn mechanics

How to Use Laniakea

Prerequisites

  • Gaming PC or next-gen console
  • Laniakea account
  • LANIA tokens for marketplace
  • 10GB storage space

Getting Started

  1. 1

    Download Client

    Download the Laniakea game client for your platform (PC, PlayStation, Xbox).

  2. 2

    Create Character

    Design your unique character and choose starting class and faction.

  3. 3

    Complete Tutorial

    Learn combat, crafting, and blockchain features through the intro questline.

  4. 4

    Join Guild

    Find a guild to access group content and territory warfare.

  5. 5

    Trade & Earn

    Craft, trade, and sell items on the blockchain marketplace for real value.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Join a guild early for better progression
  • Focus on one crafting profession
  • Save rare materials for high-tier items
  • Participate in world events for best rewards

Yes, free-to-play with optional Web3 features. Crypto enhances but doesn't gate content.
