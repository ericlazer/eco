Quick Overview

MoveWifFriends is a SocialFi platform that gamifies social interactions on Movement. Users earn tokens for making friends, creating content, and building communities.

Combining elements of friend.tech with Movement's speed, MoveWifFriends creates a sustainable social economy where genuine connections and quality content are rewarded.

Key Features

  • Tokenized social connections
  • Content monetization
  • Friend keys trading
  • Community treasuries
  • Social mining rewards
  • Reputation NFTs

How to Use MoveWifFriends

Prerequisites

  • Movement wallet
  • Twitter account
  • Active social presence
  • 0.1 MOVE to start

Getting Started

  1. 1

    Link Socials

    Connect your wallet and link Twitter to verify your identity and import connections.

  2. 2

    Buy Friend Keys

    Purchase keys of friends or influencers to access their exclusive content and chats.

  3. 3

    Create Content

    Share insights, alpha, or memes to attract key buyers and earn creator rewards.

  4. 4

    Build Network

    Grow your friend network and watch your key value increase with demand.

  5. 5

    Earn Rewards

    Claim daily rewards based on your activity, connections, and key trading volume.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Quality over quantity for connections
  • Share genuine alpha to attract buyers
  • Hold keys of rising creators early
  • Participate in community events

Frequently Asked Questions

How are key prices determined?

Prices follow a bonding curve - they increase with each purchase and decrease with sales.

Can I sell keys anytime?

Yes, but selling incurs a 5% fee and may impact your reputation score.
